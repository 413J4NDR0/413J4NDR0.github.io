Bone Tomahawk (2015)

Bone Tomahawk is a smoldering affair that builds up to a brutal climax. Blending Western and Horror, compelling performances propel a plot precariously balancing traditional Western elements with the strange and macabre in an unpredictable fashion. A modern classic.
Score: ★★★★★
Watched on Amazon Prime on 12 June 2019

Pokémon Detective Pikachu (2019)

As a fan of Pokémon since Ruby and Sapphire, I was reluctantly excited for this movie. Hollywood has not been friendly to videogame adaptations. But brilliant world building brought justice to a childhood franchise, although the plot and character development was severely lacking.
Score: ★★★☆☆
Watched at my local theatre, Century at Tanforan, with my sister on 7 June 2019
