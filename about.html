About Me

As I mentioned on my homepage, I am Alejandro Ortega, currently a senior at Duke University studying Mathematics and Computer Science.

The cover photo on my home page depicts the farm the paternal side of my family owns in Pénjamo, GTO, México, circa June 2015. That summer, my tío told me a legend that said in ancient times a giant lagartija succumbed to a deep sleep, and that its resting body formed the Sierra de Pénjamo visible in the photo. The awakening of the lagartija is said to coincide with the end of days, and will bring about the destruction of the surrounding area. Descansa en paz, tío.
